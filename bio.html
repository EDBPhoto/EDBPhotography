Evan Barber

Born and raised in Salt Lake City, Utah, Evan was fortunate to have grown up surrounded by inspirational beauty. His focus is travel and landscape photography focused on developing unique work through the combination of vintage lenses and modern tech. Evan earned Bachelors Degrees in Mechanical Engineering and Entertainment Engineering & Design from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. He is currently pursuing a Juris Doctor at The George Washington University Law School in Washington D.C. In his spare time he enjoys reading about history and politics, hiking, video editing and listening to National Public Radio.
